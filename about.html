Historical background

Bright future secondary school was established since january 2014 up todate,whereby some students joined for their ordinary level education and it has been a place where many students had the opportunity to learn and discover alot of things. As it started Mr Elias Kasmir as a director and the founder together with Mr Osward kasmir as a director and Mr malekela as the headmster and the staff members, cooperted together to make sure that students get quality education. Bright future girls' secondary school is located at Mbondole village,Msongola region.The school is about 20 kilometers from Mbagala Rangi Tatu bus terminal.

Mission and vision of the school

-Our mission is to be the center of academic excellence by providing quality education also becoming an excellent institution in Tanzania by producing graduates who are well equipped with the current needs of the 21st century like applying knowledge to authentic situation -Our vission is to be a professionally reputed learning center, known for academic excellence in Tanzania .To develop socially,culturally and professionally capable citizens.
